Ensuring Safety with Smart Gas Leak Management

About

SafeGasGuard is a smart gas leak detection system that automatically shuts off the gas regulator, cuts electricity, and activates an exhaust fan to prevent accidents. With wireless control and a manual reset feature, it ensures safety by preventing gas reactivation until the issue is resolved.
